Here Are Some More Exciting Reactions Related To Our Periodic Table

Rusting of Iron(Iron Oxide)

Fe + O2 + H2O ----> Fe2O3

Burning Of Magnesium Ribbon(Magnesium Oxide)

Mg + O2 ----> MgO

Burnt Magnesium Ribbon In Water(Magnesium Hydroxide)

MgO + H2O ----> Mg[OH]2

Displacement Reaction(In Iron)

Fe + CuSO4 ----> Cu + FeSO4

Photosynthesis

6CO2 + 6H2O ---> C6H12O6 + 6O2

Sulphuric Acid

H2SO4

Formula of Glucose

C6H12O6

Neutralization Reaction

NaOH + HCl ---> H2O + NaCl
